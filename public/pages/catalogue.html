      Help & FAQs

      What is EduCompass?

      EduCompass is your trusted platform to discover IT & Management colleges across India. We help students find the right educational institution based on their preferences, location, and course requirements.

      How do I search for colleges?

      You can search for colleges in multiple ways:

      • By Name: Type the college name in the search bar
      • By City: Enter the city where you want to study
      • By Course: Use the dropdown filter to select specific courses
      • Combine filters: Use multiple search criteria together for better results

      What types of courses are available?

      We focus on IT & Management courses including:

      • Computer Science & Engineering
      • Information Technology
      • Software Engineering
      • MBA (Master of Business Administration)
      • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
      • Data Science & Analytics
      • And many more specialized programs

      Is the college information accurate and up-to-date?

      Yes! We regularly update our database to ensure accuracy. All college information including courses, locations, and contact details are verified and updated periodically. However, we recommend contacting the college directly for the most current admission requirements and fees.

      Can I compare multiple colleges?

      Currently, you can save multiple colleges to your "Saved" list and review them side by side. This allows you to compare factors like location, courses offered, and other details to make an informed decision.

      Do you provide admission assistance?

      EduCompass is a discovery platform that helps you find colleges. For admission procedures, requirements, and applications, you'll need to contact the colleges directly. We provide contact information for each institution to help you get started.

      Is EduCompass free to use?

      Yes, EduCompass is completely free to use. You can search, save, and explore college information without any charges.

      What if I can't find a specific college?

      If you can't find a college you're looking for:

      • Try different search terms or spellings
      • Search by city instead of college name
      • Check if the college offers IT or Management courses (our focus areas)
      • Contact our support team to request adding the college to our database

      How do I contact support?

      For additional help or to report issues:

      • Email: support@educompass.in
      • Response Time: We typically respond within 24-48 hours
      • What to include: Describe your issue clearly and include any relevant college names or search terms

      Can I suggest new features?

      Absolutely! We welcome user feedback and feature suggestions. Send your ideas to feedback@educompass.in. Popular requests are prioritized for future updates.
